I have provided clickable links to how I calculated my GPA.

It is relevant because the `Universities' of NZ say that entry to Medicine is `competitive' or `selective' on the basis of GPA, primarily, but they really are not selecting candidates on this basis.

The `University' of Auckland advised me to complete a new qualification from a NZ University for purposes of eligibility. They said that if I completed a 120 point MPhil from the University of Waikato then my grades would come from previous qualifications that I had already completed. This is because the MPhil (like the PhD) is not a graded qualification. So completing a `higher research degree' does not alter your GPA.

It turns out that the Education and Training Act says that domestic adults who have attained the age of 20 years (as I had) are eligible to be enrolled in the programme that they have applied to. I applied to study Medicine, therefore I was eligible to study Medicine. I didn't need to do a new qualification for the purposes of eligibility.

But they still won't get the theses out to external examiners. They send them out for the wrong qualification (e.g., requesting `doctoral reports' to be written of a under 50,000 words masters thesis submission). They won't allow externals to sign off on completion. They won't get the updated thesis back to externals so as to prevent their signing off on qualification completion. When the only examiner based outside Australasia says to schedule an oral defence they still won't do it.

The entire time I was at the Australian National University I did not see any doctoral candidate with reports from external examiners. I did not see any doctoral candidate with a scheduled oral defence. I did not see anybody with a doctoral gown for doctoral graduation. How is it that others are claiming to have PhD's from that institution when I didn't see any evidence of anybody having reports from externals or oral defences or graduation ceremonies e.g., celebrations around University House.

How can they claim to have an education and training system when they are expediting people into Medicine (for example) when they have little to no educational background at all while refuse to acknowledge GPA's that they have awarded? How is it that my qualifications are supposed to be obsolete and expired while various Judges or University academics well over the age of 65 (the age of retirement) just keep on getting paid and will not get out.
