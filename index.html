Motivational quote for the day (or not)

Gavin Belson and Richard, on a Park Bench (Silicon Valley, Season 6, Episode 3:

It would benefit us both – equally, holy shit. Um, look, Gavin, I know we haven’t always seen eye to eye, and we’re not the best of friends, but, this might be a chance to actually help each other out. Right? What do you say?

I mean, Isn’t it obvious? I say NO!

What?

NO! Fuck no, Richard. I can’t make a move that would benefit you. You’re my competition. Help no rival. It’s one of the basic principles of business.

We just spent the last hour bonding about how this business is comprised of backstabbers and cut-throats.

Yes. Exactly. And that’s what this is. It’s a little weird you don’t get it.

I could help you, Gavin.

You already have, Richard! I mean I was sitting up here, feeling totally just impotent, and then you came along and exposed your underbelly, and practically begged me to plunge the knife in! I mean, I still have a deep hole to crawl out of, but this is a great start! Y’know, look at that, it barely hurts anymore! Well, if you’ll excuse me, I have a triathalon to train for, and I’m suddenly feeling a wellspring of energy!
