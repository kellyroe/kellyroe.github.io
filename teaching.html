Lecturing

  • Philosophy and Cognitive Science, Course design, Lecturing, tutoring, grading, Macquarie University, 2010.
  • The Department Chair told me what textbook to prescribe for this course. I do not now remember what the book was. I wrote the lecture slides as a walk-through to help unpack or explain the content of the text-book. The last topic was an exception. I introduced the students to current research being done in psychology at Macquarie. Their understanding of this was not assessed as they were busy writing their final essay on topics already covered. It was not my decision to teach from that textbook.
